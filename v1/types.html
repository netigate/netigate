Types and enums

Types

ISO 8601 dates

All dates are referenced to UTC, and specified according to ISO 8601. 

2016-09-27T15:38:26Z

ISO 639-3 language codes

All languages are specified according to ISO 639-3.

SMS sender

Maximum of 11 characters. Only a-z, A-Z and 0-9 allowed.

SMS recipient

Maximum of 15 digits, formatted with the country code first, i.e. 46735123456
